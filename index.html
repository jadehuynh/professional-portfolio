About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Jade Huynh, and I am currently a student studying Full Stack Development. Originally from small town Minnesota, I finished high school and attended the University of Minnesota for Interior Design. Shortly after classes began, I recognized it was not the right field for me. I then enrolled in Le Cordon Bleu to become a pastry chef, propelling my passion for desserts! Fortunately, I was able to cook in wonderful kitchens with talented chefs all over the Twin Cities for over a decade. I also am an Origami Artist, professionally creating fine art for seven years as well. Now I am interested in redirecting my path to encounter to challenges and gain more knowledge in the tech field. I am eager to learn, and looking forward to what the future brings with the determination I have to succeed in new territory.
